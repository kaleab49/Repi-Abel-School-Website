About Us

At Repi Abel School, we are committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating educational environment where every student can thrive. Located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, our school offers a comprehensive curriculum, dedicated teachers, and state-of-the-art facilities to support the holistic development of our students.

We provide great services and education.

Repi Abel School is located in the Kolfe-Keranio zone, Wordea 4 in the Addis Ababa region of Ethiopia. Our mission is to inspire and empower students to achieve academic excellence, foster a lifelong love of learning, and develop the skills and character necessary to contribute positively to society. Academic Programs We offer a diverse range of academic programs designed to meet the unique needs and interests of our students: Elementary School: Our elementary program focuses on building foundational skills in reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies. Middle School: In our middle school program, students explore a broader range of subjects and begin to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills. High School: Our high school curriculum prepares students for college and beyond with a rigorous academic program, Advanced Placement (AP) courses, and opportunities for career exploration.
